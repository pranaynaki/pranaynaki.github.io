Courses

Coming into University, I honestly didn't know what a terminal was (dark times indeed),
but apart from just imparting knowledge, the CS classes here have
strongly emphasized independent inquiry, a skill which I'm very grateful to have.

C.S. Classes

Current

Operating Systems (439) - So far, our projects have included dealing with concurrency issues between multiple processors, and implementing a heap along with malloc/free.

Artificial Intelligence (343) - We will be using OpenNERO for projects.
