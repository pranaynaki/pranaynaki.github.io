Markov Chain Text Generator

This text generator takes a text file as input and generates a stream of output that resembles the original work in its style of writing. The output even seems to follow grammar rules correctly (for the most part). How does it do that?? Well, a markov chain is first constructed using the input. The chain can optionally be trained on any characteristic of the text file, but I have defaulted it to words for simplicity. States for this chain are sequences of 3 consecutive words, and a directed graph is constructed between all possible states. A random state is then picked to generate an output stream, from which the next state is generated, and so on.

Go ahead and try it! Either click the GENERATE! button below (defaults to one of my favorite books), or paste your own url pointing to a .txt file. A good place to find one is the Higher Intellect Database.
